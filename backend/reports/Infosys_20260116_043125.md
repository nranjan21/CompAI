## Company Overview
Infosys Limited, headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India, is one of the Big Six Indian IT companies, providing a wide range of services including IT consulting, business process management, and digital transformation solutions. Founded in 1981 by seven engineers, including N. R. Narayana Murthy and Nandan Nilekani, Infosys initially started with a capital of $250 and went public in 1993, becoming the first Indian company listed on Nasdaq. Over the years, the company has expanded its offerings and established subsidiaries such as Infosys Consulting and Infosys BPM, focusing on various domains including financial services, healthcare, and manufacturing.
